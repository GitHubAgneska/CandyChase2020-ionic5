Rules

Halloween night is finally here... And it's time for some serious hunting !!

  • 1. Select your age to get your map
  • 2. Start your quest in your neighborhood
  • 3.Find the candy of the list and put it in your backpack:
    The points will get you through LEVELS with TRICK or TREAT challenges, And CARDS to get !
    You can also save your favorite addresses !
  • 4. After the hunt, you can check in the candy details if the candy you found are a good fit for you
    ( in case you have allergies or a special diet for example )
    If this is not the case, you can trade this/these candy for some other ones with your friends!

Good luck and let the hunt begin !!!
